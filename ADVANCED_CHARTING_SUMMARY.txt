    ↓

    ↓

    ↓
Fetch Volume Analysis → /api/chart/volume-analysis
    ↓
Complete Page Render

ACCESSING THE FEATURE

================================================================================
API ENDPOINTS
================================================================================

GET /api/chart/ohlcv
Parameters: symbol, timeframe
Response: Array of candlestick data with OHLCV

GET /api/chart/indicators
Parameters: symbol, timeframe
Response: All 50+ indicators by category

GET /api/chart/support-resistance
Parameters: symbol, timeframe
Response: Pivot points and support/resistance levels

GET /api/chart/volume-analysis
Parameters: symbol, timeframe
Response: Volume statistics and OBV/MFI
